Uzbek Plov (Lamb and Rice Pilaf)

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups long-grain rice (such as basmati)
  • 1 lb (450g) lamb or beef, cubed
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 large carrots, julienned
  • 1 head of garlic, cloves peeled and whole
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 4 cups hot water or broth
  • Fresh herbs for garnish (parsley, cilantro)

Steps

Prepare Ingredients:

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water.
  2. In a large skillet or pot, saut√© the chopped onion and minced garlic until softened.

Cook Ground Meat:

  1. Add the ground meat to the skillet and cook until browned.

Add Vegetables and Spices:

  1. Stir in the grated or sliced carrots, cumin powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for a few minutes until the carrots soften

Add Rice:

  1. Add the rinsed rice to the pot and mix well with the meat and vegetables.

Pour in Broth:

  1. Pour the broth over the rice mixture. Bring it to a boil.

Simmer

  1. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a lid, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.

Fluff and Serve:

  1. Fluff the rice with a fork to separate the grains.
  2. Serve the easy plov hot.
