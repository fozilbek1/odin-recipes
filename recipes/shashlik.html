Shashlik (Kebab)

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs (700g) of meat (beef, lamb, or chicken), cut into bite-sized cubes
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinega
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Wooden or metal skewers, soaked if wooden
  • Fresh lemon wedges for serving
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

Prepare Ingredients:

  1. Cut the meat into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Cut the onion and bell pepper into chunks.

Assemble Skewers:

  1. Thread the meat, onion, and bell pepper onto skewers, alternating between them.

Season:

  1. Brush the skewers with vegetable oil and season with salt and pepper.

Grill:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Grill the skewers for about 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the meat is cooked through and has a nice char.

Serve:

  1. Remove the skewers from the grill.
  2. Serve the easy shashlik hot.
