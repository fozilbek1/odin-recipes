Manti

Ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • Water (as needed)

For the Filling:

  • 1/2 lb (225g) ground lamb or beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For the Serving:

  • Greek yogurt
  • Garlic yogurt sauce (yogurt mixed with minced garlic and a pinch of salt)
  • Sumac (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

Fill Wonton Wrappers:

  1. Mix ground meat with chopped onion, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place a small amount of the mixture in the center of each wonton wrapper.

Fold and Seal:

  1. Fold the wonton wrapper over the filling to create a triangle.
  2. Seal the edges by pressing with your fingers.

Cook Manti:

  1. Steam the filled wontons for about 10-15 minutes until cooked through.
